Your Perfect Maritime Getaway

Escape to Island Road Cabin, nestled in the charming village of Malagash on Nova Scotia's scenic North Shore. Recently renovated with love and care, our cozy retreat comfortably sleeps 5 guests and offers the perfect base for exploring the warm waters and coastal beauty of the region.

Just minutes from the vibrant restaurants and pubs of nearby Tatamagouche, you'll have the best of both worlds - peaceful evenings away from it all, and easy access to local Maritime culture and cuisine.
